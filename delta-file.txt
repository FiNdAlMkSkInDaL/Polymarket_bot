feat(config): add 17 new strategy parameters for all 4 volume expansion vectors

V1 — Maker routing:
  maker_routing_enabled=True — globally enables maker-order fee path.
  maker_eqs_discount=0.85 — lowers EQS threshold by 15% for maker entries (50→42.5).

  confluence_eqs_floor=35.0 — hard floor for threshold after applying discounts.
  confluence_min_factors=2 — minimum active factors to unlock any discount.
  confluence_whale_discount=5.0 — discount when whale strong-confluence present.

  drift_signal_enabled=True — activates MeanReversionDrift detector.
  drift_lookback_bars=10 — cumulative displacement window.
  drift_z_threshold=1.0 — minimum |displacement| to fire.
  drift_vol_ceiling=0.015 — EWMA σ ceiling (low-vol only).

  probe_sizing_enabled=True — enables micro-sized probe entries below threshold.
  probe_eqs_floor=35.0 — minimum score for a probe tier to be considered.

  probe_max_usd=2.0 — hard cap on probe position USD value.
